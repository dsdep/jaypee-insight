Welcome To Jaypee Insight

A college is a highly interactive environment and the collaboration between various entities is an integral 
part of its functioning.This collaboration not only takes place in the classroom but also regularly between 
management and the students,placement coordinator and the students.With increase in the number of 
courses,teachers and students in an institution,the traditional methods of information sharing like
notice boards do not serve purpose efficiently.That's why Jaypee Insight is introduced.

 Jaypee Insight is an interaction portal whose objective is to enhance the communication and
 interaction between different levels in the college.It is very helpful for the students of JIIT-128.
 Jaypee Insight serves various functionalities like teachers can upload assignments along with 
deadline for submission,teachers can also upload reference material for the students,teachers can also
maintain the record of the students who have submitted the assignments and who have not,students can 
also access the time table and academic calendar,students can also submit assignments online to the 
respective teacher,students can also view the information regarding any upcoming events and workshops
in the college campus.
