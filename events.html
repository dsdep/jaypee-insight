JIIT Events

Our College organizes many events in the fields of sports,art,technical,music and dramatics. Our College has separate societies for each 
category of events. These hubs or societies manage all their respective events from taking auditions of to planning the dates on which the 
event will be held. Students take part in these events and win amazing prices. Our College Bhangra and Dramatics Team also participate
